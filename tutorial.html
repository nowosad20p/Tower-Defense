The goal of the game

The goal of the game is simple - you have to protect your castle from opponents that appear in red portals.

The course of the game

To protect your castle you must build towers that will attack enemies. Towers must be placed in special places. Killed enemies give you money that allows you to build new towers and upgrade existing ones.

Towers

There are four types of turret: mortar, fire, ice and archer's tower. Each of them has different stats, and has unique powerful evolutions that increase their power.
  • Mage's Tower - by dealing damage to the enemy, it sets it on fire, which causes it to receive additional damage spread over time.
  • Ice Tower - Damaging an enemy slows them down - this effect is cumulative!
  • Archer's tower - Shoots the fastest and farthest, but does not apply any special effects.
  • Mortar - Deals massive area of ​​effect damage.

End of the game

If you manage to kill all waves of enemies you win, but if you loose all of your hp you loose. Good luck have fun!
