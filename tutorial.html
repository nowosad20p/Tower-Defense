The goal of this game is simple - you have to protect your castle from enemies, which spawn in red portals.
In order to win game you have to build and upgrade towers on special slots. They will fight for u and kill enemies - dead enemies drop gold, which u can use to build even more towers.
There are four types of towers: fire, ice, archer and mortar. Fire tower sets opponents on fire, dealing damage in time, ice tower slows enemies, mortar tower deals splash damage and archer towers shoot fast and penetrate armor. Each tower has own stats and evolutions, which makes them even more powerfull.
If you manage to kill all waves of enemies you win, but if you loose all of your hp you loose. Good luck have fun!
